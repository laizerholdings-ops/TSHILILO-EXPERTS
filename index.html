Professional Services

Expert Installation

Our veterans manage the scientific installation of your instant lawn. This involves precision alignment to avoid visible seams and mechanical rolling to eliminate air pockets, ensuring immediate root-to-soil synergy for rapid growth and a carpet-like finish. We handle everything from residential porches to massive sports fields.

National Delivery

Freshness is the key to root viability. We operate a specialized logistics network that ensures your grass is harvested and delivered to your site within 24 hours. We serve residential neighborhoods, office parks, and commercial developments across South Africa with temperature-monitored transit systems.

Structural Prep

A great lawn starts underground. Our preparation process includes deep-root weed sterilization, organic nutrient infusion, and high-precision mechanical leveling. This scientific foundation prevents future undulations and ensures your selected variety thrives in its new environment.
